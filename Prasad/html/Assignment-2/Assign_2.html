From the garage to the Googleplex

The Google story begins in 1995 at Stanford University.
Larry Page was considering Standard for grad school and Sergey Brin, a student there, was assignment to show him around.

By some acount, they disagreed about nearly everything during that first meeting, but by the following year they struck a partnership.
Working from their dorm rooms, they built a search engine that used links to determine the importance of individual pages on the World Wide Web.

They called this search engine Backrub.

Initial public offering

Google's initial public offering (IPO) took place five years later, on 19th August 2004.
At that time LarryPage, Sergey Brin, and Eric Schmidt agreed to work together at Google for 3020years, until the year 2014.
