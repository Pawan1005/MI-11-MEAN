About Us

AmberSoft is a leading provider of permanent recruitment, contract professionals and talent management solutions nationwide. AmberSoft is a recruitment product & recruitment services company. We help our clients achieve greater organizational performance by assessing, recruiting and developing the best and brightest people for their business. AmberSoft was earlier known as KnowledgeQuest Infotech. In that life we have been doing recruitment, software development as well as product development. Our software business was acquired by a US company called sTec in March 2011. Our CEO Anurag Agarwal, was heading India operations for sTec. In May, 2013, he decided to move out of sTec and restart the company. And in this life we have taken name AmberSoft.
