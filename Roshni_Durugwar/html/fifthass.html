From the garage to the googleplex

The Goggle story begin in 1995 at Stanford University.
Lary page was considering Stanford for grad school and Sergey Brin,a student
there, was assigned to show him around.

By some accounts,they disagreed about nearly everything during that first meeting,but by the following
year they struck a Partnership.
Working from their dorm rooms,they built a search engine that used links to determine the importance of individual pages on the World Wide Web
They called this earch engine Backrub.

Initial public offering

Google's initial public offering(IPO) took place five years later,on 19th August 2004.
At that time Lary Page, Sergey Brin, and Eric Schmidt agreed to work together at Google for 30 20 years,until the year 2024.
